# Spaghetti Carbonara

**Ingredients:**
- 200g spaghetti
- 2 eggs
- 100g pancetta
- 50g Parmesan cheese
- Salt & pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water.
2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese.
4. Drain the pasta and mix everything together.
5. Enjoy!
